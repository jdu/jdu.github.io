2022 Wellcome Data Tech Round-Up

Below is the first, inaugural tech round-up for the Wellcome Data team.

A bit of a tour-de-force of all the different tools, languages, systems, services and applications that we are using, have used, or abandoned in the last year in our constant striving towards excellence.

The Good

Back to Airflow and Away from Argo

We used Argo for the last couple of years after a short stint using Airflow for some ad-hoc processes.

In undertaking a complete rebuild of our data platform and pipelines, it fast became apparent that the semantics around Argo were really hard to reason about when you got into more complex pipelines similar to what we were dealing with. Some of the other pain points of Argo i’ll get into further down.
